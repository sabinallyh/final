About me

My name is Sabina, I am a Stockholm based UX-design student at Nackademin. User Experience to me is not looking at the shape or colour of a button,but ensuring that design and products are tailored to solve user needs and problems. That is the essence and key to good UX design.

Apart from studying UX, I have an immense interest in graphic design which I like to incorporate. With that being said, I do know and am very aware of when to put my interest in UI aside, to ensure a foundation of well built UX.
